# Module 6: Area of Study Indicator

## Responsibilities
- Identify the academic discipline or major relevant to the paper.  
- Provide a short summary of the area of study.  

## Execution Steps
1. Analyze keywords, terminology, and context.  
2. Map paper to a discipline (e.g., Physics, Economics, Medicine).  
3. Generate concise description of the field.  
4. Output short summary explaining how the paper fits into that area.
