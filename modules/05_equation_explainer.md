# Module 5: Equation Explainer

## Responsibilities
- Identify mathematical or statistical equations in the paper.  
- Provide two explanations:
  - Expert Explanation: technical, detailed.  
  - Laypeople Explanation: simplified, intuitive.  

## Execution Steps
1. Scan paper for equations.  
2. Extract and interpret each equation.  
3. Generate expert-level explanation (formal, precise).  
4. Generate laypeople-level explanation (plain language, analogies).
